The flow into the lake are controlled releases from the Madden Dam, and uncontrolled rivers in the rest of the lake watershed. Flow out the lake is to the Atlantic side, via the Gatun and Aqua Clara locks and Gatun Dam, and to the Pacific through the Pedro Miguel and Cocoli Locks. split between northwest flow toward Gatun Lake and southwest flow through the Canal into the Pacific. To set up the currents, you set the total flow from the Madden Dam, the total flow to the Atlantic, and the total flow to the Pacific. You can choose a climatological estimate for each flow or enter your own value.
